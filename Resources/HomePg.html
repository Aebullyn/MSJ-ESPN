Mission Statement

Mission Sports Programming Network (MSPN) exists to amplify student athletics by creating an accessible, engaging platform that highlights every sport, every team, and every athlete. While Mission athletes are already celebrated, MSPN brings their stories, performances, and moments to a wider audience. We hope to foster school spirit through a more fun, interactive sports experience for everyone. Fantasy sports leaguesow available!
