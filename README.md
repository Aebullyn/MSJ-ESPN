Mission ESPN: MSPN
- An all-in-one place to watch livestreams of games, post-game interviews, support teams, make donations, and more. This app is a version of ESPN for Mission San Jose Athletics! Accessed by HomePg.html
- Java classes aren't linked due to limitations of programs, but both java and html are fully functional independently.
- Further back-end exploration of advanced programming would allow our company to further develop this product, which as of now is manually updated.

Fantasy Sports Feature
- You select your own team of players in your sport of choice, setting a lineup every week. Then, you watch as they score points, all of which are worth fantasy points. Every week, you are matched up against someone else in your league, and whoever has the most fantasy points that week, wins!
